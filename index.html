Who We Are

D-Precise Medical Laboratories / Blood Bank was founded in 2011. We are fully registered with Lagos State Blood Bank and the Medical Laboratory Council of Nigeria.

Over the years, we have supplied numerous hospitals directly and through logistics companies like LifeBank. Our new blood bank in Lekki is dedicated to serving you better.

  • Founded in 2011, over a decade of experience.
  • Registered with the Medical Laboratory Council of Nigeria.
